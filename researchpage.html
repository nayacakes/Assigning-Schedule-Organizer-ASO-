About Our Study

This page is made to describe and explain the purpose and concept of 12 ESTEM-C7's Group 1 research title. As well as elaborate the concept of this website for respondents' further knowledge about it.

Assessing the Effectiveness of Assigning Schedule Organizer in the productivity of Selected 12ESTEM-C Students in University of Cabuyao (UC-PNC) A.Y. 2023-2024

Concept:

This research study investigates the impact of implementing the Assigning Schedule Organizer (ASO.com) on the productivity of 12ESTEM-C students at University of Cabuyao (UC-PNC). By examining the effects and benefits of ASO.com utilization, the study aims to address key questions regarding students' motivation, task organization, discipline, and entertainment, as well as the benefits related to attention span, academic responsibilities, and time management skills. Through quantitative analysis and correlation assessment, the study seeks to determine the significant relationship between the effectiveness and benefits of ASO.com usage on student productivity. Results aim to provide insights into the efficacy of ASO.com as a tool for enhancing student productivity and inform educational practices for improved learning outcomes.
